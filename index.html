The Opportunity

Your brand has outgrown
what Etsy can carry.

Sakina Scrubs is not just an apparel brand. Through products like the Al Huda Hijab, you are solving a specific and underserved problem: Muslim and Arab women in healthcare have long lacked workwear that supports both modesty and professional function. Your background as a nurse, your participation in entrepreneurship programs, and the media recognition you have already earned are the ingredients of a brand with real credibility and real staying power.

Your Etsy storefront has proven that demand exists. But Etsy has a ceiling. It can sell a product. It cannot fully carry a brand story, build a loyal community, or give you the long-term digital foundation that a business like Sakina Scrubs deserves.

This project is about closing that gap and giving your brand a home that matches its ambition.

Phase 1 Scope

Everything included
in this engagement.

Phase 1 establishes your independent digital presence. Every element is designed to strengthen the brand, support your audience, and create a foundation for long-term growth.

01
Custom Brand Website

A premium, fully custom website experience that presents Sakina Scrubs as a serious brand. Not a template. Not a marketplace listing. A digital home designed around your story, your products, and the problem you solve.

02
Product and Shop Pages

Dedicated pages showcasing your products with the depth and quality they deserve. Purchase pathways link to your established Etsy checkout during this phase, preserving your current fulfillment process without disruption.

03
Integrated Blog

A blog published directly under your own brand and domain. Your space for founder updates, stories from women in medicine, product education, clinical insights, and community announcements. Content that builds long-term visibility and authority.

04
Community Comments

Blog posts include an integrated comment section designed to create space for genuine discussion. Built to favor meaningful engagement and help build a more invested audience around the brand.

05
Editorial Dashboard

A private administrative dashboard where you can write posts, upload images, edit content, and publish updates independently. No code, no developer required for day-to-day content management.

06
Mobile-Optimized Design

Designed and built to perform beautifully across all screen sizes, ensuring a strong experience whether your audience finds you on a phone, tablet, or desktop.
